My Design Process

I follow a user-centered approach to design

1

Research

Understand user needs, business goals, and technical constraints through interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis.

2

Define

Synthesize research findings into user personas, journey maps, and clear problem statements.

3

Ideate

Generate multiple solutions through brainstorming, sketching, and collaborative workshops.

4

Prototype

Create low to high fidelity prototypes to test and validate design concepts.

5

Test

Conduct usability testing with real users to gather feedback and iterate on designs.

6

Implement

Work closely with developers to ensure design integrity in the final product.
